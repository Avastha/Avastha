The Project

Two Minds,
One Frequency

Avastha is a collaborative project born from two distinct musical worlds colliding into a single, powerful live experience.

Rooted in original, performance-driven material, Avastha delivers precise, high-energy psytrance crafted for the live experience. By fusing their original productions with enhanced tracks from leading artists through custom edits and additions, the duo ensures a unique experience tailored for any dance floor.

The name itself — Avastha — speaks to the shifting landscape of life: wakefulness, dreams, deep sleep, and the awakening beyond. Every set is that journey.
